About Me

I am 19 years old from Layton Utah. I am currently attending BYU-Idaho majoring in web design and development. I have always loved photography and specifically drone photography. I am familiar with adobe photoshop and lightroom and am capable of taking quality pictures and editing them. You can check out my work here

High Quality

The drone I use is the DJI mini 3 pro. The mini 3 pro can shoot 4k video along with 48MP photo resolution. This drone is more than capable of taking high quality pictures and a variety of features that allow me to get the perfect shot.

Certified

As a certified Part 107 operator accredited by the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), I bring professional expertise to aerial photography. Ensuring compliance with FAA regulations, I am authorized to capture stunning aerial shots for commercial purposes.
